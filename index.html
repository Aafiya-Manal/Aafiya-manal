Research & Exploration

Questions I'm
investigating.

Student AI Behavior Study

How do students actually use AI?

An original peer research study exploring how students interact with AI tools in learning environments — and what that reveals about the gap between AI availability and AI literacy.

MethodSemi-structured peer interviews
ParticipantsMiddle & high school students

Key Findings

  • Heavy reliance on AI for answers without understanding
  • Weak personal time management systems
  • Lack of AI literacy among students
  • Strong need for guided, structured learning tools

Design Goal

Build tools that encourage depth.

The research shapes my product thinking — every feature I design aims to encourage productive, responsible technology use and help students develop real understanding rather than surface-level answers.
